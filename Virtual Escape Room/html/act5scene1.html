Act 5 Scene 1
“I see through your lies. I will escape you!”

You yell at the apparition in spirited defiance. The laughing and vision fade away, and the trail to freedom reappears before you, clear as day. With a weak and weary grin, you set off down the trail with a determined rev of the engine. As you ride, you find your senses slowly dulling. The roar of the engine softens to a muffled groan, and the snowy landscape blurs into a mixed palette of white and green. The smell of fresh pine and pristine snow is replaced with a sickly sweet floral scent: frangipani, you finally identify.

“I must just be exhausted. This has been nothing short of traumatising, and my mind needs rest.”

Too lethargic to resist, you allow yourself to drift into a restful trance.
