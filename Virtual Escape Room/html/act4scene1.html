Act 4 Scene 1
You rush excitedly into the shed, and your lungs are pervaded by a sleet of wood dust hanging in the air. Hacking and coughing, you take in the state of the three snowmobiles before you. Two lie crippled and lame, missing either treads or engine parts. Only one sits poised and ready, a chariot ready to carry you to victorious escape. However, with a sigh of dismay, you realise the keys are nowhere to be found. You will have to somehow hotwire the engine.
