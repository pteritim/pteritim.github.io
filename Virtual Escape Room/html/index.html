Introduction

Welcome to the NUS Rover Scouts virtual event! The following are some ground rules for the activity:

  1. These webpages have been optimised for Google Chrome. Please ensure that you are using this browser, otherwise certain elements may not work.

  2. Please use a laptop or desktop for this activity. Formatting may not render correctly on smaller screens.

  3. Internet access is allowed throughout the activity.

  4. This activity consists of a series of webpages with puzzles for you to solve, all linked by a horror-themed narrative.

  5. You have 90 minutes (1.5h) to complete the activity.


Have fun!
