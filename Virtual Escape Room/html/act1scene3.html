Act 1 Scene 3
You awake an hour later to the sound of a muffled rustling next to your ear. Reaching up, you find a tattered note with a strange script on it. While the text is foreign, the handwriting is familiar. Although clearly hastily written, the chicken scratchings are reminiscent of Igor’s famously terrible handwriting. As the memory of what caused you to pass out surges back into your mind like a tidal wave of horror, you realise that the note might contain a clue as to what happened to your friends.
