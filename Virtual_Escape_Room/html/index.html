Introduction

Welcome to the NUS Rover Scouts virtual event! The following are some ground rules for the activity:

  1. If you encounter any technical issues, try reloading your page or clearing your cache.

  2. Please use a laptop for this activity. Formatting was not adjusted for smaller screens.

  3. Feel free to consult the Internet throughout the activity.

  4. This activity consists of a series of webpages with puzzles for you to solve, all linked by a horror-themed narrative.

  5. If you are truly stuck, you can advance through the story by manually editing the Act and Scene number in the URL.


Have fun!
